Notation guide

Notes: C D E F G A B (middle octave) · c d e… = octave up · C, = octave down · c' = octave up again · ^C = sharp · _B = flat · =C = natural · z = rest · | = barline
Length: C2 = 2 beats · C/2 = half beat · C3/2 = dotted · Headers: T:Title K:G M:3/4 Q:120 L:1/8
